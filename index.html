Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystal balls below. By clicking on a crystal ball you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal ball is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each crystal ball will change.
